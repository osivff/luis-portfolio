About me

Luis Fitch

Architectural Technician based in San Diego, California

I'm a 29-year-old Architectural Technician and proud San Diego native, with formative life experience in Mexico that broadens my design perspective. A graduate of San Diego Mesa College, I’ve built a solid foundation in architectural design and technical drawing, enhanced by years of hands-on collaboration with contractors in the field.

Fluent in a range of architectural software, I bridge the gap between practical field knowledge and digital design execution. Beyond the drawing board, I’m an avid surfer and dog dad to a golden retriever, sharing life’s adventures with my wife. In my spare time, I explore personal design concepts—pushing boundaries, refining ideas, and staying curious about how architecture shapes the world around us.
